Enora About Me Research Projects

(2021)Tiwi Language Resources:
Tiwi is an Australian Aboriginal language spoken by Tiwi People. According to the 2016 census, there are currently 2,040 speakers meaning that it is a critial time for language documentation work. I have been working on developing language technology for Tiwi including a transcription keyboard layout and a morphological transducer
(2020)Rock Climbing Hold Classifier:
I built a convolutional neural network in tensorflow to classify images of indoor rock climbing gym holds into 6 categories: edges, jugs, pinches, pockets, slopers, crimps.
