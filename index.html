Enora
Hello! My name is Enora and I am a PhD student in the NALA lab and LECS lab at CU Boulder. I received my bachelor's degree from Bryn Mawr College in 2022 with a double major in computer science and linguistics. Currently, I work on natural language processing for low-resource/marginalized languages with a particular interest in computational morphology and typology. In my free time, you can find me holding open hours at the media archaeology lab, picking apples with the community fruit rescue, or sewing clothes with materials found at art parts.
